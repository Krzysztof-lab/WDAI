Privacy Policy

Welcome to SkyPhotos! We care about your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. We strive to ensure that your experience on our platform remains safe and transparent.

What Data Do We Collect?

We collect information you provide directly, such as contact information (name, surname, email address) when using our services, for example, when signing up for a newsletter or contacting us through a form. We may also gather data automatically, like IP addresses, to improve the functionality of our site.

How Do We Use the Data?

We use your data to improve the quality of our services and tailor our offerings to your preferences. We may also use the data to contact you for marketing purposes if you have given your consent. Additionally, we analyze aggregated data to better understand user behavior and enhance user satisfaction.

What Are Your Rights?

You have the right to access your data, correct it, delete it, or restrict its processing. We are here to support you in exercising these rights effectively and efficiently. If you have any questions or wish to exercise these rights, please contact us.

Security

We care about the security of your data and apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access. Regular audits and data encryption are part of our commitment to data protection.
